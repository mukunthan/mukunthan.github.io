About Me

I am a senior lecturer and AI consultant specializing in interpretable machine learning, IoT, and digital healthcare systems. I serve at the University of Jaffna and was a research associate at King’s College London. My PhD focused on antimicrobial resistance prediction using explainable AI models at the University of Surrey. I have also worked with LSEG Technology and served in multiple international R&D collaborations.

Research Interests & Collaborations

  • Explainable AI for healthcare and disease prediction
  • IoT-driven diagnostic platforms and embedded systems
  • Collaborations with Oslo University Hospital, Lancaster University, Vidiia, and University of Surrey
  • ADB-funded Smart Grid AI Model leadership

Academic & Professional Experience

  • Senior Lecturer – University of Jaffna (2023–Present)

Key Projects
