I am working on interpretable multi-modal Lung cancer prediction as Post Doctoral Researcher at Comprehensive Cancer centre, King's College , London since September 2022. I am also working as Machine Leanring consultant at SenzMate , Sri Lanka. I completed my PhD at the Centre for Vision, Speech, and Signal Processing (CVSSP), University of Surrey, UK, focused on developing interpretable machine learning models to predict antimicrobial resistance. Before my PhD research, I worked as Lecturer (Probationary) at the University of Jaffna, Sri Lanka, and as a Senior Software Engineer in LSEG Technology , Sri Lanka. I am broadly interested on applying explainable artificial intelligence techniques in healthcare. I am seeking opportunities to utilise my expertise and skills to make meaningful contributions and provide leadership in these areas. With multi-disciplinary backgrounds, I am committed to fostering innovation and propelling the field of digital healthcare forward through my research and teaching endeavours.
